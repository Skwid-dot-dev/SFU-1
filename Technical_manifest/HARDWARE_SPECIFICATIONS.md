Title: Component Requirements & Industrial Standards

1. Rearing & Cultivation Hardware
Larvae Trays: Food-grade HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene), 600mm x 400mm x 150mm.
Sifting System: Automated vibrating screen separator (1.0mm mesh for frass, 3.0mm for pupae separation).
Hydroponic Racks: 5-tier vertical flood-and-drain system with 450nm/660nm (Blue/Red) LED spectrum lights.

2. Processing & Milling
Blancher: Industrial steam-jacketed blancher (Target: 100°C for 90 seconds).
Dehydrator: Heat-pump assisted low-temperature dryer (Target: <10% moisture content).
Impact Mill: 5HP Stainless Steel Burr Mill (Target: <150-micron particle size).

3. 3D Food Printer Specs (The Fabricator)
Extrusion Type: Positive Displacement Paste Extrusion.
Motors: NEMA 23 High-Torque Steppers with 10:1 planetary gearboxes.
Nozzle: 4.0mm – 8.0mm Interchangeable Food-Grade Stainless Steel.
Print Bed: Heated (60°C) to assist in "setting" the Agar/Guar binders.
